
Daily Feminism is a digital archive of feminism that documents the development of mainstream feminist movements, their key figures, and core viewpoints, and helps users explore their own feminist values ​​through interactive quizzes.
In public discourse, "feminism" is often misunderstood, simplified, or even stigmatized. I hope that by organizing theoretical, historical, and intellectual texts, more people can directly engage with feminism itself, rather than with the stereotypes surrounding it.
This is an attempt at recording, organizing, and disseminating information.
This project, completed using Figma Make and Codex, employs a collage art style (my recent favorite). Atlas presents users with seven major feminist schools of thought, followed by a Likert scale to create a personal feminist profile. The developmental history section explores the essence, limitations, and breakthroughs of each theory. As the website is informational, access to related resources is provided for detailed reading. The spectrum section helps users intuitively understand the connections and contradictions between different schools, emphasizing that feminism should not be isolated. The final reading room encourages users to take the time in this fast-paced, short-video era to read a complete theoretical book. While the website aims to lower the barrier to entry for feminism through interactive and innovative design, providing a basic understanding, a more immersive reading experience is still necessary for a deeper comprehension.

Daily Feminism 是一个女性主义数字档案馆，收录主流女权主义流派的发展历程、代表人物与核心观点，并通过互动测试帮助用户探索自己的女性主义价值取向。
在公共讨论中，“女权主义”常常被误解、简化，甚至污名化。我希望通过整理理论、历史与思想文本，让更多人能够直接接触女性主义本身，而不是围绕它的刻板印象。
这是一次关于记录、整理与传播的尝试。
该项目使用figma make + codex 完成，采用拼贴画的艺术风格（我近期最爱的风格），atlas向用户展示七个主流女权主义流派，而后用户可通过完成一个李克特五级量表获取自己的女权主义画像，紧接着来到发展历程板块，了解每个理论的内涵/局限/突破，由于网站属于信息收录性质，有资料网站入口可供用户详细阅读。光谱则能帮助用户更加直观地了解各流派之间的联系与矛盾之处，因为女性主义不应该是孤立的。最后的阅读室仍然期待用户能够在快节奏的短视频时代能够沉下心来，试着去完整阅读一本理论书籍，因为本网站旨在通过互动和耳目一新的设计降低接触女性主义的门槛，让用户有个简单的认识，如果用户想要有更深层次的理解，仍需要完整的沉浸式阅读。
